Overview

"Car" is a massive 12-part racing franchise created primarily by Sreedev, with creative contributions from Sriram. Spanning releases from 2022 to 2026, the series follows the fictionalized characters of Sriram, Sreedev, and Gopi as they race to win prizes in a high-energy, comic style.

Box Office History

  • The First Era (2022): The first 10 films were released in rapid succession throughout 2022 and were considered major commercial hits.
  • The 11th Installment (2025): Released after a production hiatus, this entry boasted a massive budget of $290 million. While it earned a whopping $568 million, the "blockbuster" status was declined due to the exorbitant production costs, rendering it an "average hit".
  • The Future (2026): The 12th and potentially final installment is currently scheduled for a release in late May 2026.

Overview

Originally titled Oru KSRTC Apaaratha, "One Bus Tale" is a comedy-action franchise created by Sreedev. The saga revolves around the chaotic lives of Jeron (a bus driver) and Gopi (the bus conductor), along with passengers Sriram, Abhinav, and Anay, who manage to live on the bus for free.

Performance & Reception

The franchise consists of 6 movies with widely varying critical reception, though all remained profitable.

Overview

The MAD MC Universe (also known as MAD MC Movies) is a tetralogy created by Sriram, released between 2021 and 2023. Unlike traditional franchises, these four films share no narrative continuity. Instead, they are thematically linked by their focus on "mad" or mentally eccentric protagonists navigating chaotic, unpredictable storylines.

Filmography

1. Memen Kochu

Plot: A disturbed child burns his uncle's motorbike after being denied a toy car, prompting the uncle to seek revenge.
Duration: 2 hours 31 mins | Letterboxd Rating: 3.8/5

2. Memen Kochu 2

Plot: The conflict escalates as the child burns the uncle (causing ~30% burns) after being struck, leading to further chaos.
Duration: 2 hours 27 mins | Letterboxd Rating: 3.7/5

3. Dogwater Sumesh

Plot: Follows "Dogwater Sumesh," a lunatic aspiring singer who is humiliated by a whitewashed musician and decides to seek vengeance.
Duration: 1 hour 54 mins | Letterboxd Rating: 3.8/5

4. Arana Ser

Plot: Follows Arana Ser (Arun P), a tech CEO with severe dementia. When a new employee, Sumesh, joins via a referral from David, he attempts to cure Arana's condition. However, the SRS (Srielandian IRS) raids the company for unpaid taxes dating back to 2012, forcing them to fight off the government.
Duration: 2 hours 22 mins | Letterboxd Rating: 4.0/5

Franchise Overview

The Lover is a massive franchise created by Gopi, blending high-octane action with romance.

The Beginning

The 1st movie was a huge box office hit, grossing $1 Billion against a budget of $100 Million.

The Dark Ages

The subsequent Lover movies (2 through 55) were largely considered box office bombs and were heavily criticized by audiences and critics alike.

The Redemption (Movie 56)

The 56th movie, the newest installment, earned positive reviews and became a certified hit, reviving interest in the long-running saga.
